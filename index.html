🚩 Clause 3.2 — INDEMNIFICATION HIGH RISK

"Contractor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Company... from any and all claims... regardless of whether such claims are caused by Company's negligence..."

💡 THE FIX: Add "mutual" + cap liability at total project fee

💡 THE FIX: Require 30-day notice on BOTH sides

"This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms..."

💡 THE FIX: Change to opt-in renewal, remove automatic clause
